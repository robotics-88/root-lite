Navigation

Mouse Controls

  • Left click and drag to pan the scene.
  • Scroll to zoom in and out.
  • Rotation
    • RIGHT CLICK and DRAG to select an object and rotate around it

      If a point is not detected, the camera will rotate around the current view

  • Press 'b' to undo last camera movement

Touch Controls

  • Single finger drag to pan the scene.
  • Two finger drag to rotate the scene.
  • Pinch to zoom in and out.
